MEMORANDUM 
With reference to his/her application for recruitment to the post of Stenographer 
Grade -II in CBDT on the basis of Stenographer Grade 'C' & 'D' Examination 2020, 
the candidates selected as Stenographer Grade -II in CBDT are directed to 
indicate/exercise his/her prefere~ces, in order of priority for allotment of 18 regions 
under Income Tax Department (CBDT) all over the country, in the prescribed format. 
The PREFERENCE FORM duly filled/complete in all respects, may be sent URGENTLY 
via email onrecruitmentcell.hrd @incometax .gov.inlatestby14 .11.2022. However, later the 
hard copy of the preference form may also be sent through SPEED POST/REGISTERD 
POST or COURIER/BY HAND or PERSONALLY at the following address: 
Deputy Director of Income Tax (CMD-I), 
Directorate of Income Tax (HRD), 
2nd floor, Jawahar Lal Nehru Stadium, 
Gate No.1, New Delhi -110003. 
2. Candidate are cautioned that if, for any reasons, he/she fails or does not indicate 
his/her preferences in said FORMAT for whatever reasons by 14.11.2022 the 
REGION/STATE will be allotted to him/her, on the basis of merit-cum-preference, by this 
Directorate and NO REQUEST for change of allotted region will be entertained at any stage 
of recruitment. 
End: As above.
