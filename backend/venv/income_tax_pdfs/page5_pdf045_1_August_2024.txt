Order under proviso to sub-section (5) of section 1448 of the Income-tax Act, 1961 specifying 
the circumstances for the purposes of enquiries or verification functions by the Verification 
Unit-regarding. 
In pursuance of the proviso to sub-section (5) of Section 144B of the Income-tax Act,1961 
(hereinafter referred to as "the Act"), the Central Board of Direct Taxes hereby specifies the 
following circumstances, for the purpose of enquiry or verification functions referred to in Section 
144B(3)(iii) of the Act by the Verification Unit: 
I. Non-availability of digital footprint in respect of the assessee or any other person. 
II. Electronic or Online verification is not possible on account of no response to notice issued 
to the assessee or any other person. 
III. Physical verification of assets or premises or persons is required, regardless of the presence 
of digital footprint. 
2. This order shall come into force with immediate effect.
