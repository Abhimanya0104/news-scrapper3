MEMORANDUM • 
With reference to his/her application for recruitment to the post of Inspector of 
Income Tax in CBDT on the basis of Combined Graduate Level Examination 2023, the 
candidates selected as Inspector of Income Tax in CBDT are directed to indicate/ exercise 
his/her preferences, in order of priority for allotment of 18 regions under Income Tax 
Department (CBDT) all over the country, in the prescribed formant. The PREFERENCE 
FORM duly filled/ complete in all respects, may be sent URGENTLY VIA EMAIL ONLY 
on recruitmentcell.hrd@incometax.gov.in latest by 26.12.2023. 
2. Candidate are cautioned that if, for any reasons, he/she fails or does not indicate 
his/her preferences in said FORMAT for whatever reasons by 26.12.2023 the 
REGION/STATE will be allotted to him/her, on the basis of merit-cum -preference , by this 
Directorate and NO REQUEST for change of allotted region will be entertained at any stage 
of recruitment. The candidates are directed to submit ONLY ONE PREFERENCE FORM 
and no change in preferences would be allowed. It may also be noted that INCOMPLETE 
FORMS WOULD BE REJECTED and not be considered for the purpose of allocation.
