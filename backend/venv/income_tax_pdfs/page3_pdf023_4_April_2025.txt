Subject: Allocation of region to the candidates selected for the post of Inspector of 
Income Tax in CBDT through Combined Graduate Level Examination, 2024-Regarding. 
Allocation of region to the candidates selected for the post of Inspector of Income 
Tax in Central Board of Direct Taxes (CBDT) through Combined Graduate Level 
Examination, 2024 is enclosed. 
2. Furthermore, the selected candidates are directed to address any concerns related to 
appointment formalities and extension in joining time to the concerned appointing 
authority (CCAs) only. It is important to note that the allocation of regions is final, and no 
requests for re-allocation will be entertained by this Directorate. 
3. This issues with the prior approval of Competent Authority.
