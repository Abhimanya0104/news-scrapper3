MEMORANDUM 
Subject: Revised Allocation of region to the candidates selected for the post of 
Inspector of Income Tax in CBDT through Combined Graduate Level 
Examination, 2023-Regarding. 
In supersession of this office Memorandum of even number dated 16.01.2024, 
revised Allocation of Region to candidates selected to the post of Inspector of 
Income Tax in Central Board of Direct Taxes (CBDT) through Combined Graduate 
Level Examination , 2023 is enclosed as Annexure-A. 
2. Further, the candidates are directed that any representation related to 
appointment formalities and extension in joining time may be made to the concerned 
appointing authority (CCAs) only. It may further be noted that the allocation is 
final and no request for re-allocation of region will be entertained by this 
Directorate. 
3. Candidates selected for the post of Inspector of Income Tax in CBDT through 
CGLE 2023 are hereby informed that their appointment is subject to the outcome of 
W.P (C) No. 16593/2023 filed by Shubham Pal & Drs. before the Hon'ble High 
Court of Delhi.
