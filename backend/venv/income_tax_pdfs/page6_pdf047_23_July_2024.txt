Proposed Changes in Tax 
Rates1

--- Page 3 ---
No changes are proposed in old tax regime.
Under the new tax regime as given in Section 115BAC, following tax rates are proposed:Changes in Tax Rates
S. No Income Range Tax Rate
1) Up to Rs. 3,00,000 Nil
2) From Rs. 3,00,001 to Rs. 7,00,000 5%
3) From Rs. 7,00,001 to Rs. 10,00,000 10%
4) From Rs. 10,00,001 to Rs. 12,00,000 15%
5) From Rs. 12,00,001 to Rs. 15,00,000 20%
6) Above Rs. 15,00,000 30%
The tax rate for foreign companies is proposed to be reduced from 40% to 35%.
