Madam/Sir, 
I am directed to refer to CBDT decision in file of even number dated 
09.05.2024 on the above subject and to communicate the following: -
Ministerial Staff Examination -2024 
(a) The Departmental Examination- 2024 for the Ministerial Staff shall be held 
in accordance with the Departmental Examination Rules for the Ministerial 
Staff 1998. 
(b) The schedule of Examination is as per Annex-I. 
(c) Following categories will be eligible to appear in Ministerial Staff 
Examination 2024 -
1. T.A. 
2. Steno Grade I, II 
3. LDC including Hindi Typist 
4. Notice server (having passed Computer Test) 
5. MTS (having passed Computer Test)

(d) The effective date of passing the Examination shall be governed by this

(e) The Pro CCsIT/CCsIT/Pr. CsIT/CsIT(In-charge of Examination) may enrol
