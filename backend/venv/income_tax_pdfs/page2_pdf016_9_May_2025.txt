NOTIFICATION 
 
New Delhi, the      of    May, 2025 
 
 
G.S.R. …….—In exercise of the powers conferred by the proviso to article 309 of the constitution, the 
President hereby makes the following rules to further amend the Ministry of Finance, Department of 
Revenue, Central Board of Direct Taxes, Office Superintendent ( Group ‘B’ post), Recruitment Rules,2024, 
namely:- 
 
1. (a) These rules may be called the Ministry of Finance, Department of Revenue, Central Board of 
Direct Taxes, Office Superintendent (Group ‘B’ post), Recruitment (Amendment), Rules,2025. 
 
(b) They shall be deemed to have come into force with effect from 20.09.2024  i.e. the date of 
publication of Ministry of Finance, Department of Revenue, Central Board of Direct Taxes, Office 
Superintendent ( Group ‘B’ post), Recruitment Rules,2024 in the Official Gazette vide G.S.R 126. 
 
2. In Schedule to the Ministry of Finance, Department of Revenue, Central Board of Direct Taxes, 
Office Superintendent (Group ‘B’ post), Recruitment Rules,2024, in column 8  for the entries, the 
following amendment shall be made, namely- 
 
Column-8 
 
Whether age and educational qualifications prescribed for Direct Recruits will apply in the 
case of promotees:       
 
        Age: No 
        Educational Qualification: No 
 
EXPLANATORY MEMORANDUM 
 
Principal Rules were published in Part-II Section 3(i) of the Gazette of India vide G.S.R. No. 126 dated 
20th September 2024. This amendment is as per guidelines of  DoPT OM No. AB.14017/48/2010-Estt (RR) 
dated 31st December 2010 and 20th September 2022. 
 
It is also certified that the amendment from a retrospective date will not affect any one adversely as similar 
provision existed in the earlier rules.
