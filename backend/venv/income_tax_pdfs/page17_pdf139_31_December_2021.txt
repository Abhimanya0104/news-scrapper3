2. Para 1 I) TDS Charges : Any verification or survey u/s 133A of the Act by the TDS charges

Para 1 iii) International Taxation Division : The TDS surveys by the International

(IT&TP) / CCIT (IT&TP) as one member of the collegium and CCIT (TDS) / 
jurisdictional CCIT of TDS charge or the Pro CCIT of the region where there is no

mem ber of the collegium . The survey shall be conducted by the officers of TDS 
charge by including officers of IT&TP Division in the team. For any other survey 
by the International Taxation Division, the same is to be approved by the 
collegium of Pr.CCIT (IT&TP)/ CCIT (IT&TP) and DGiT (Inv.). Such surveys shall

IT&TP Division in the team.
