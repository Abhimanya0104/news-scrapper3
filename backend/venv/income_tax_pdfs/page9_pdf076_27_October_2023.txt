(Final Selection of Young Professional under Young Professional 
Scheme, 2023 in Rajasthan Region) 
The Screening and Selection Committee have taken the interview of 
the candidates on 25.10.2023 & 26.10.2023. 
Total 19 candidates were called for the interview. Out of the 19 
candidates, 15 candidates have appeared for interview before the Screening 
and Selection Committee . The Committee has recommended to select Miss 
Ritika Agrawal for the post of Young Professional. The Committee has also 
selected Shri Akash Aggarwal in the extended panel in case Miss Ritika 
Agrawal does not join the post or leaves the job within a year.
