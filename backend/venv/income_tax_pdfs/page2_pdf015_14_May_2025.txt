Ministerial Staff Examination -2025 
(a) The Departmental Examinations 2025 for the Ministerial Staff shall be held 
in accordance with the Amended Departmental Examination Rules for the 
Ministerial Staff 1998 (Effective from 2023 examination onwards). 
(b) The schedule of Examination is as per Annex-I . 
(c) Following categories will be eligible to appear in Ministerial Staff 
Examination 2025 -
1. T.A. 
2. Steno Grade I, II 
3. LDC including Hindi Typist 
4. Notice server (having passed Computer Test) 
5. MTS (having passed Computer Test)
