With reference to his/her application for recruitment to the post of Tax Assistant in 
CBDT on the basis of Combined Graduate Level Examination 2021, the candidates 
selected as Tax Assistant in CBDT are directed to indicate/ exercise his/her preferences , 
in order of priority for allotment of 18 regions under Income Tax Department (CBDT) all 
over the country, in the prescribed formant. The PREFERENCE FORM duly 
filled/ complete in all respects, may be sent URGENTLY via email on 
recruitmentc ell.hrd@incometax .gov.in latest by 29.03.2023.
