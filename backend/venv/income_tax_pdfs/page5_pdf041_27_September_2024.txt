NOTIFICATION 
Subject: Notification of 'Designated Authority' under the Direct Tax Vivad Se Vishwas 
Scheme, 2024 -reg. 
The Central Board of Direct Taxes, in exercise of the powers under sub-section (1) and 
(2) of section 120 of the Income-tax Act, 1961, have conferred jurisdiction upon the undersigned 
vide notification no. 51/2014 in F. No. 187/35/2014-ITA-I dated 22.10.2014, published in the 
Gazette of India, Extraordinary in Part-II Section 3, sub-section (ii) No. S.O. 2753(E) and as 
amended from time to time. 
Further, in exercise of powers conferred upon the undersigned by clause (e) of sub­
section (1) of section 89 of the Finance (No.2) Act, 2024 [under the Direct Tax Vivad Se 
Vishwas Scheme, 2024], I, the Principal Chief Commissioner of Income-tax, UP (East), 
Lucknow, hereby notify that the following jurisdictional Principal Commissioners of Income­
tax/Commiss ioners of Income-tax as specified in column (2), having their headquarters at the 
place specified in the corresponding entries in column (4) shall be the 'Designated Authorities' 
for the purposes of the said clause, in respect of the 'declarant ' over whom the jurisdiction is 
exercised.
