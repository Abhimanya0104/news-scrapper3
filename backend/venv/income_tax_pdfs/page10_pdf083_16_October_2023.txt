(Screening Criteria and List of Shortlisted Candidate) 
1. The Screening Criteria determined for shortlisting of application 
form is as under:-
The eligibility criteria for the screening of applications are determined 
as under: 
S. (i) Indian Nationals holding Graduate / Post Graduate degree in Law 
from recognized Universities, Colleges and Institutions of National 
and International repute, and or Chartered Accountant. 
(ii) Candidates should have obtained at least 50% marks in the 3 Year 
LLB or 5 Year integrated LLB Degree program or Post graduate 
degree program or a qualified Chartered Accountant.
