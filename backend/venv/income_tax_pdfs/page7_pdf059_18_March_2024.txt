New Delhi, the 18th March, 2024 
Subject: Order under section 119 of the Income-tax Act, 1961-reg. 
The Financial Year 2023-24 closes on 31 st March, 2024, which is Sunday. Further, 
30th March, 2024 is a Saturday and 29th March, 2024 is a closed holiday. 
Therefore , to facilitate completion of pending departmental work, all the Income Tax 
Offices throughout India shall remain open on 29th, 30th and 31 st March, 2024. This direction 
is issued for administrative convenience by the Central Board of Direct Taxes in exercise of 
powers conferred under section 119 of the Income-tax Act,1961.
